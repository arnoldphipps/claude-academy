## THE DUOLINGO FORMULA — What Makes Them #1

Duolingo's success comes from **6 core mechanics** that create habit loops:

### 1. Daily Streaks (3x return rate)
- Track consecutive days of practice
- Users who hit 7 days are 3.6x more likely to stay long-term
- Streak Freeze reduced churn by 21%
- **We need this: Track days practiced, show streak prominently in sidebar**

### 2. XP & Leaderboards (40% more engagement)
- We already have XP — but no leaderboard
- Weekly competitions drive 25% more lesson completions
- Double XP events create 50% activity surges
- **We need this: Weekly leaderboard, global and company-level**

### 3. Badges & Achievements (30% completion boost)
- Milestone rewards at key moments
- "First Lesson", "Perfect Score", "5-Day Streak", "Module Complete"
- **We need this: Achievement badge system with shareable images**

### 4. Push Notifications ("Don't break your streak!")
- Perfectly timed reminders boost engagement 25%
- "You're on a 7-day streak — don't stop now!"
- **We need this: Email-based for now, push notifications when we have a mobile app**

### 5. Social Features (viral growth)
- Friend challenges, shared progress, invites
- Users who connect with friends have 2x retention
- **We need this: Invite friends, share achievements on LinkedIn**

### 6. Micro-Learning (habit formation)
- 5-minute bite-sized daily sessions
- Low commitment = higher consistency
- **We need this: Daily 5-minute challenge alongside the full lessons**
